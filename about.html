A public benefit corporation

Evidence in the public interest

ForEvidence.ai exists to build the independent evidence capacity the AI era needs — and to make sure that capacity serves the organizations and people affected by AI, not only those selling it.

Our mission

Make AI's claims answerable to evidence

The last decade taught us to build powerful AI. This one has to teach us to trust it — responsibly, and with proof. That requires evaluation and assurance evidence fabric — both infrastructure and workflows — built in from the start, as well as the re-skilling and certification of the people on the loop.

We build that capacity three ways: as products, as engagements, and — through the public-goods layer — as a free commons built with nonprofit, academic, and foundation partners.

Why a public benefit corporation?

Independent evidence is a public good. We are a public benefit corporation working alongside nonprofits, academic centers, and foundations — so the free layer stays in public hands while the commercial work sustains it.
