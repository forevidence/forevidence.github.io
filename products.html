Product 01

Evidence Fabric

Weaves the evidence you're already producing into one auditable record — without replacing anything you use.

AI solution/model cards live in one repo, evaluations in a spreadsheet, sign-offs in email, monitoring in a dashboard nobody exports. When a regulator, customer, or board asks "show me," teams scramble to reassemble a story from fragments.

Evidence Fabric isn't another platform to install. It connects to the tools your teams already use — and to the governance features your cloud, model, and orchestration vendors already ship natively — and weaves what they produce into one continuous, versioned evidence record.

One question it answers instantly: "What evidence do we have that this system is safe, compliant, and performing — as of today?"

⊕

No installation

Connects to what you already run instead of replacing it. Nothing to rip out, no migration, no new system of record.

⬚

Vendor-neutral

Works across whichever model, cloud, orchestration, and governance tools you've chosen — or are still evaluating.

⊞

Platform-native friendly

Complements the governance capabilities your platforms already ship. It reads them; it doesn't duplicate or compete with them.

⇄

Decision-proof

Because it doesn't depend on your vendor choices, it survives them — including switches made mid-selection.

▤

Capture

Pull evidence from your existing tools — repos, eval runs, monitoring, approvals — instead of duplicating work.

⬡

Structure

Map every artifact to a control or competency in your chosen framework, so gaps are visible at a glance.

◉

Verify

Flag stale, missing, or contradicted evidence automatically — assurance is a living state, not a one-time audit.

◷

Version

Every record is timestamped, so you can show what was known and true at any point in time.

⇱

Export

Generate an evidence pack for a regulator, customer, or board in minutes — not a two-week fire drill.

◆

Connect

Feeds from our evaluation and assurance work, and from your systems' observability.

Product 02

Evidentia assessment infrastructure

The technical backbone certification bodies run their credentials on.

Evidentia is the assessment infrastructure credential programs run on. A serious AI-oversight credential can't be tested with multiple-choice questions. It has to assess whether someone can actually evaluate an agent, configure a guardrail, and read an observability dashboard.

Evidentia is the infrastructure that makes that possible — built on open primitives so it stays portable, and paired with a public register so any credential issued on it is verifiable by anyone. The aim is the oversight workforce AI deployments quietly assume: the person ready to take over, and the local validators every adopting site needs before it can trust a system in its own population.

How we build programs on it
◎

Evaluation harnesses

Reproducible environments for testing agent behavior, tool use, and edge-case handling.

▦

Guardrail labs

Hands-on environments where candidates configure and defend real safety controls.

◉

Observability dashboards

Live monitoring surfaces candidates must read, interpret, and act on.

✓

Badging & public register

Issuance plus a public, checkable registry — verification as trust infrastructure.
