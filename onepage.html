Our approach

Everything traces back to evidence

Whatever the engagement, our method is the same: define what "good" means in your context, gather evidence against recognized standards, and leave behind an auditable trail anyone can review. It's the spine that runs through every solution — and it's what the name means.

  • Standards-anchored: NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, and domain canons
  • Reproducible evaluation harnesses and documented methodology
  • Evidence packaged for regulators, boards, and the public alike

Evidentia · Credentialing partnerships

Build and run rigorous AI credentials, together

Evidentia is our assessment infrastructure for credential programs. We partner with certification and accreditation bodies to develop the content and operate the assessment infrastructure behind their AI credentials — you own the standard, we build and run the program.

The LEED model for AI competence: one body authors the standard, a separate operator delivers the credential. Built on open primitives (vendor-neutral, portable), independent and firewalled from what we build and assess, with outcomes-based exams and crosswalks to mandate (EU AI Act, accreditation).

What Evidentia includes

  • Innovator Track — co-design the credential, mapped to recognized standards
  • Creation Module — content plus practical-exam infrastructure
  • Program management — delivery, proctoring, badging, refresh
  • Register & verification — a public, checkable credential registry
Why we exist

Evidence in the public interest

As AI systems multiply, the scarce resource isn't models — it's trustworthy evidence, and the independent, credentialed capacity to appraise AI rather than take it on faith.

ForEvidence.ai is a public benefit corporation working alongside nonprofits, academic centers, and foundations to keep that capacity in public hands — independent of the vendors who profit from the technology.

Our commitments

  • Methods anchored to recognized public standards
  • Transparent, reproducible, reviewable evidence
